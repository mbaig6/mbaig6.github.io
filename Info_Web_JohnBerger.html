John Berger Ways of Seeing

  • Europeans believed their civilization was more advanced
  • Oil paintings were very popular and expensive
  • European oil paint symbolized the ability to buy, to furnish, and to own.
  • The painting themselves was simple demonstrations of what gold and money can buy
  • Paintings were a demonstration of the artists’ virtuosity and the buyer’s wealth
  • “The oil painting & publicity image have much in common”
  • “When everybody’s place in society is more or less determined by birth, personal envy is a less familiar emotion”
